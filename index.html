Rewards

The currency of rewards is LifePoints (LPs). LifePoints are awarded at a rate of 1 LP per 1 minute spent at a fitness facility. There are four tiers of LifePoint rewards: Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum; based on 7, 10, 15 and 22 hours spent in the fitness facility, respectively.
